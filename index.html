🧮 The Key Formulas

From the elliptic curve spectrum:

E₀ = -166.3335 (ground state)

Δ = 7.2429728916 (level spacing)

6|E₀|/Δ = 137.788863

The fine structure constant:

α = 1/(6|E₀|/Δ - 3/4 - Δ/2528)

Lepton masses (with Δ in MeV):

m_e = Δ × α × (π + 2)

m_μ = m_e × (6|E₀|/Δ) × 3/2

m_τ = m_μ × (6|E₀|/Δ) × 5/41

m_H = m_τ × (6|E₀|/Δ) / 2
